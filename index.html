Building & Renovation Services — Cape Town

Everything your home needs, under one team.

01
Brick Work & Paving

Brick laying for all jobs big or small — new builds, renovations, extensions, fireplaces, patios and retaining walls. Solid, lasting work.

02
Plastering & Crack Repairs

Indoor and outdoor plastering for whole homes or single walls. We find the root cause of cracks, repair properly, and leave a clean finish.

03
Screeding & Wooden Floors

Floor screeding to level and prepare surfaces. Wooden floor sanding and re-sealing to bring tired floors back to their original quality.

04
Plumbing

Reliable plumbing repairs and installations for bathrooms, kitchens and swimming pools. Leaks, blockages, full installations — available 24 hours.

05
Tiling & Cladding

Wall and floor tiling for bathrooms, kitchens, outdoor areas and feature walls. We also install cladding and stone veneers.

06
Painting & Waterproofing

Exterior and interior painting, roof painting, damp proofing and waterproofing. We repair leaking roofs, patios, bathrooms and showers.

07
Skimming & Ceilings

Smooth skim plaster applied to walls and ceilings for a paint-ready surface. Ceiling installations, repairs and board replacements.

08
Drywalling & Partitioning

Drywall for internal walls, partitions, design features, arches and eaves. Quick, clean and cost-effective space division.

09
Electrical Services

General electrical work including lighting, downlights, DB boards, appliance installations and swimming pool pumps.

10
Built-in Cupboards

Custom built-in cupboards and storage solutions for bedrooms, kitchens and living areas. Designed to fit your space.

11
Bathroom & Kitchen Renovations

Complete remodels from gut to finished — waterproof, tile, plumb and paint. We handle the whole job so you don't have to manage five contractors.

12
Construction & Extensions

New rooms, garages, house extensions and full construction projects across Cape Town. Built properly, from the ground up.
